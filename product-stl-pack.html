  • Infill Density: 20-25%
  • Print Speed: 50mm/s
  • Wall Thickness: 1.2mm (3 perimeters)

Material Recommendations

PLA (Recommended)

Easy to print, good for most cases and enclosures. Perfect for beginners.

PETG

More durable than PLA, chemical resistant. Great for tool holders.

ABS

Heat resistant, strong. Good for automotive or high-temp applications.

Post-Processing Tips

  • Light sanding may be needed for perfect fit
  • Use a drill to clean out screw holes if needed
  • Test fit before final assembly
  • Consider acetone vapor smoothing for ABS prints
